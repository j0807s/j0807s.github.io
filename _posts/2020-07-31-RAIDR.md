* #### Modified the simulators and made DDR4 configuration files for the simulators
* #### Used bloomfilter to store the information of weak rows
* #### Used DRAMSim2 and gem5 as simulators with memory intensive benchmarks from SPEC CPU2006
* #### Checked RAIDR averaged 12% performance improvement over auto-refresh scheme in DDR4
